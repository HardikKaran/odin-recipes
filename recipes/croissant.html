Description

A buttery, crescent-shaped French pastry recognised by its flaky, golden-brown crust and layered interior.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients
  2. Chill initially for 2 hours
  3. Thin out the butter onto parchment paper
  4. Shape into layers and chill
  5. Roll into shape and cut
  6. Gently brush with egg wash
  7. Bake at 190C for 20 minutes

Description

Pizza originated from Italy made of a doughy base with a tomato sauce layer and meat/vegetables of cook's choice.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll dough into a ball
  2. Turn dough until flat plate
  3. Spread tomato sauce
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Spread pepperoni pieces
  6. Put in oven for 30 minutes
  7. Cut into 8 pieces and serve.

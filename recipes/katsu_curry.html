Katsu Curry

Home

Description

Katsu Curry originitated in Japan famous for it's spicy seasoning and flavourful aroma.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Golden Curry
  • Cooked Rice
  • Spring Onions

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into tiny strips
  2. Batter chicken in flour and egg wash
  3. Fry chicken until golden brown
  4. Cook 1 cup rice in rice cooker
  5. Boil curry in pot for 15 minutes
  6. Put all in one plate
  7. Cut spring onions into small pieces and garnish
